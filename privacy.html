Privacy Policy

Gymbar · last updated 23 July 2026

Gymbar does not collect any data. Nothing you enter leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Gymbar has no account system, no server, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not receive your workouts, supplement records, settings, device identifiers, or usage statistics — because the app never sends them anywhere.

What the app stores

Your training plan, workout history, supplement records and settings are written to your device's local storage and stay there. They are included in your own device backups (iCloud or encrypted local backup) if you have backups enabled — that copy is controlled by Apple and by you, not by us.

Export and sharing

Settings → Export data creates a JSON file and hands it to the system share sheet. Where that file goes is entirely your choice. The app does not upload it.

Deletion

Deleting Gymbar from your device deletes all of its data. There is nothing stored elsewhere for us to delete, and no request to send us.

Children

Gymbar is rated 4+ and, since it collects no data, it collects no data from children either.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published on this page with a new date. A change that introduced any data collection would also be described in the app's App Store privacy details.
